Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich acrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt bitter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butterb has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of pan to the centre every 20 sec ( as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
